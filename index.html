Hi, I’m Rich

I help people build communities of high trust, deep belonging, and shared purpose.

I’m the co-founder of Loomio (collaborative decision-making software), Enspiral (a legendary self-managing collective), Microsolidarity (a network of mutual-aid communities), and The Hum (organisational design for non-hierarchical teams).

These days my focus is Casa Tilo, a retreat centre and co-living space in the forests near Barcelona, where we treat hospitality as a sacred practice, convening the kind of people who change your life.
