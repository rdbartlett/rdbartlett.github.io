A gathering for people who know each other from Twitter to hang out and enjoy the exquisite pleasures of being surrounded by likeminded weirdos with high openness and no particular objectives besides weaving social fabric. And it's Rich's birthday!

Accomodation

We're staying in a big old Andalusian farmhouse with a courtyard, balconies, a swimming pool, and views over the Granada countryside out towards the Sierra Nevada mountain range.

There are 10 bedrooms, 7 bathrooms, 3 living rooms and 3 kitchens. So we'll fit about 20 of us, depending on how many people share a bed.

It's a 200 year old house that's been recently renovated so it's a pretty lush place.

Food

Every effort will be made to provide healthy and filling food for everyone. Dietary requirements must be included as part of your booking. Guests will be expected to participate in cooking and cleaning.

Activities

While the "official" structured events tend to be very light touch the gathering is designed around communal living, with cooking and cleaning for each other being the main activity. Once the chores are done, there is a great deal of Unstructured Time for unwinding, relaxing, hanging out, chilling, and seeing what magic comes from a few day's intensive conversation.

There will be an opening ceremony and a closing ceremony, and we'll generate a timetable of activities in an "unconference" style - participants both suggest and lead the events they want to see, so there will be plenty of activities to participate in, from lockpicking to clowning, from Fight Club to Epistemics.

Extended stay

Rich & Nati are living in this house throughout the winter. So we're happy to have a few guests stay for the week following RichFest. You can indicate your interest in this on the registration form and we'll be in touch.

Travel

The venue is El Almendrillo in Fuente del Conde. (Google Maps link.)

Driving
Google Maps may send you on a crazy impassable path. The correct access is via the slightly less crazy driveway that starts at the village church.

The house is about an hour's drive from Malaga Airport. Let us know if you're willing to rent a car at Malaga Airport and help shuttle other people to the event. We'll cover the cost.

Flying
Fly to Malaga airport, take the airport train (12 min) to the central station (Málaga María Zambrano), then take the fast train to Antequera (20 min). We'll arrange transport from Antequera (it's a 30 min drive to the house).

Train
There's a fast train Madrid to Antequera.

Other bits

If there's anything we should know so we can host you well, reach out. The house is unfortunately not equipped for wheelchair users. If you have other accessibility needs, let us know and we'll do what we can.

Sad to say, this event is not going to be well equipped for kids. Hopefully RichFest2 will be bigger and better and we can accomodate families next time.
